Simple Initiative Tracker

  • Fill out your parameters and click the button to add an actor.
  • You can remove actors by clicking the button in the upper right hand corner of that actor's row. Alternatively, you can clear all actors by clicking the button.
  • HP can be altered by clicking the and buttons on the right and left of the actor's HP. Alternatively you can alter HP by clicking on the HP value and typing in a new value.
  • Press the button to advance turns.
  • Clicking will toggle the title of the page, and input form for easier viewing on mobile.
  • The button is currently under construction, and functionality will be added soon.
