Simple Initiative Tracker

  • adds an actor if the proper parameters have been populated.
  • in the upper right hand corner of an actor's row will remove that specific actor.
  • removes all actors.
  • and alter the HP in increments of one. Clicking on the HP value will allow you to change it freely.
  • advances turns. A red glow indicates the current actor's turn.
  • toggles the title and input form on and off for easier viewing on mobile.
  • brings up the advanced options to toggle automatic initiative rolls, multiple actors, and initiative modifiers.
